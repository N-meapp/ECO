Kochi

Planning a trip to Kochi? Choose from our flexible travel packages, perfect for short getaways, family trips, or luxury holidays. Enjoy delicious food, private cab services, comfortable stays, and an exciting cruise. Savor authentic Kerala cuisine, relax in cozy hotels, and travel stress-free in well-maintained cabs. Experience a serene backwater cruise and an exclusive voyage aboard the Minar Cochin Cruise Ship, offering panoramic ocean views, fine dining, and world-class hospitality. Whether exploring historical sites, relaxing on beaches, or immersing in Kochi’s culture, we ensure a seamless and memorable journey for you!

Alappuzha

Planning a trip to Alappuzha, the Venice of the East? Our exclusive packages offer a perfect blend of serene backwaters, lush greenery, and golden beaches, whether for a houseboat cruise, romantic escape, or family vacation.

Enjoy a luxury houseboat stay with freshly prepared authentic Kerala cuisine, unwind at Alappuzha Beach, explore hidden canals on a Shikara ride, and visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary & Pathiramanal Island.

Let us handle the details while you relax, explore, and create unforgettable memories in Alappuzha!

Thekkady

Explore Thekkady, a paradise for wildlife and nature lovers. Home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, it offers thrilling safaris, lush spice plantations, and serene landscapes.

Our Thekkady travel packages include: Wildlife Safari – Spot exotic animals on a Periyar Lake boat ride. Spice Tour – Discover Kerala’s famous spices. Trekking & Nature Walks – Enjoy scenic trails. Authentic Kerala Cuisine – Savor traditional flavors. Elephant Ride – Add adventure to your journey.

Experience Thekkady’s beauty with a seamless, well-planned getaway!

Kanyakumari

Discover Kanyakumari, where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean converge. Famous for its breathtaking sunrises, sunsets, and cultural heritage, it’s a must-visit destination.

Our Kanyakumari travel packages include: Sunset View – Witness stunning ocean sunsets. Vivekananda Rock Memorial – Explore this iconic spiritual landmark. Kanyakumari Temple – Visit the revered pilgrimage site. Thiruvalluvar Statue – Admire the towering tribute to the poet. Scenic Boat Ride – Sail to Vivekananda Memorial Island.

Whether for spirituality, scenic beauty, or relaxation, Kanyakumari offers unforgettable memories!
